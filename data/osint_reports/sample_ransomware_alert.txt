RANSOMWARE ALERT — LockBit 3.0 Variant "LockBit-NG"

Severity: CRITICAL
Date: 2024-01-15

Overview:
A new variant of LockBit 3.0 ransomware, internally designated "LockBit-NG" (Next Generation), has been detected in active campaigns targeting healthcare and manufacturing sectors. The variant introduces improved evasion techniques and faster encryption speeds.

Technical Details:
- Initial Access: Exploitation of Citrix NetScaler vulnerability CVE-2023-4966 ("Citrix Bleed")
- Execution: PowerShell-based loader (T1059.001) downloads encrypted payload
- Defense Evasion: Process injection (T1055) into legitimate system processes
- Impact: AES-256-CTR encryption of local and network-shared files (T1486)
- Ransom: 2-5 BTC demanded via Tor-based payment portal

Indicators of Compromise:
- Payload MD5: a1b2c3d4e5f6a1b2c3d4e5f6a1b2c3d4
- Payload SHA256: 7d865e959b2466918c9863afca942d0fb89d7c9ac0c99bafc3749504ded97730
- C2 IP: 203.0.113.50
- Ransom Note Filename: README-LOCKBIT.txt
- Tor Payment URL: lockbitng[.]onion/payment
- Mutex: Global\LockBit_NG_2024

Affected Products:
- Citrix NetScaler ADC and Gateway (unpatched)
- Windows Server 2016, 2019, 2022
- VMware ESXi 6.7, 7.0

Recommended Actions:
1. Patch CVE-2023-4966 immediately
2. Isolate affected systems from network
3. Verify backup integrity (offline/immutable backups)
4. Deploy EDR with ransomware-specific detection rules
5. Report incidents to CISA and FBI IC3
