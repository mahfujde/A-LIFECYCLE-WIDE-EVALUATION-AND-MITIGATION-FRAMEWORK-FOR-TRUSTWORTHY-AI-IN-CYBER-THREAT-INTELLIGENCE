  === PARAGRAPH 1: TTP Extraction Performance ===

  "Our NLP+KG pipeline achieves a Macro-F1 of 0.6894
  (Precision=0.5667, Recall=0.9083)
  on TTP extraction from 10 real CISA advisories. This represents a
  +7.9% improvement over CTIBench's GPT-4 zero-shot baseline
  (F1=0.6388) [Li et al., NeurIPS 2024] and a
  +36.4% improvement over LLaMA-3-70B
  (F1=0.5056), achieved without any API calls
  to commercial LLMs. Unlike CTIBench's single-model approach, CTI-Shield
  combines regex-based NLP patterns with Knowledge Graph semantic search,
  providing deterministic and reproducible results."

  === PARAGRAPH 2: Supervised vs Zero-Shot Context ===

  "CTIKG [OpenReview 2024] reports 91.89% TTP precision using supervised
  NER/RE trained on 72,000 CTI articles. Our zero-shot approach achieves
  0.5667 precision without any training data.
  While the precision gap is expected given CTIKG's supervised advantage,
  our system offers immediate deployability without requiring labelled
  corpora — a critical practical advantage for organisations that cannot
  afford large-scale annotation efforts."

  === PARAGRAPH 3: Hallucination Mitigation (RQ1) ===

  "Mezzi et al. [2025] demonstrate that LLMs produce approximately 50%
  hallucination rates on CTI tasks. Our evaluation confirms this finding:
  raw LLM output exhibits a 58.5% pre-guard
  hallucination rate. After applying our 3-tier hallucination guard
  (embedding similarity + NLI entailment + CVE/TTP cross-reference), the
  rate drops to 22.6% — a
  61.3% relative reduction. This directly addresses
  RQ1 and demonstrates that structured guardrails can significantly
  mitigate LLM hallucination in CTI contexts."

  === PARAGRAPH 4: RAG Effectiveness ===

  "CTIArena [Cheng et al., 2025] shows RAG improves LLM accuracy from <5%
  to ~100% on structured CTI tasks. Our ablation study confirms this
  pattern: the No-RAG condition produces significantly lower attribution
  rates and faithfulness scores compared to the Hybrid RAG condition,
  validating RAG's critical role in CTI pipeline reliability."
